Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sunshine Cafe started as a small coffee shop with a passion for quality ingredients and warm hospitality. Over the years, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood spot known for our delicious food, specialty coffees, and welcoming atmosphere.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and take pride in preparing everything fresh daily. Our menu reflects our commitment to quality and our love for creative, flavorful dishes.
